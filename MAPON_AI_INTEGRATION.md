# MapOn.ai Integration Overview for Mansão Maromba Distribution Radar

## 🎯 **Overview**
MapOn.ai transforms your Notion databases into live, interactive maps without requiring GIS expertise. This integration provides visual insights for your distribution network, competitor analysis, and regional expansion planning.

### **Distribution Mapping**
- **Current Coverage**: Visualize all active distribution points across Paraná and Brazil
- **Regional Gaps**: Identify underserved areas for expansion
- **Performance Heatmaps**: Show sales volume by location with color-coded intensity

### **Competitor Intelligence**
- **Market Saturation**: Map competitor locations and density
- **Territory Analysis**: Understand market share by region
- **Expansion Opportunities**: Find white spaces in the market

### **Prospecting Enhancement**

## 🔗 **Integration with Your Notion Databases**

### **1. Mapa de Distribuição Mansão Maromba**
**Purpose**: Current active distribution network
**Mapping Fields**:
- **Address**: `Endereço` field for location plotting
- **Size/Volume**: `Volume Mensal` for bubble size/color intensity
- **Status**: `Status` for filtering (Ativo/Inativo)
- **Type**: `Tipo` for categorizing (Academia, Loja de Suplementos, etc.)

**Map Use Cases**:
- Distribution coverage heatmap
- Regional performance visualization
- Expansion gap analysis

### **2. Alvos de Prospecção**
**Purpose**: Prospect pipeline and competitor mapping
**Mapping Fields**:
- **Address**: Prospect location for plotting
- **Funnel Stage**: Color-code by prospect status (Lead, Contacted, Negotiating, Won, Lost)
- **Phone**: Contact information overlay
- **Rating**: If available from Google Places data

**Map Use Cases**:
- Prospect density analysis
- Conversion funnel visualization
- Geographic prospect clustering

### **3. Backlog Radar Mansão Maromba**
**Purpose**: Project planning and task mapping
**Mapping Fields**:
- **Location Context**: If tasks are location-specific
- **Priority**: Color-code by urgency (P0 Critical = Red, P1 High = Orange, etc.)
- **Status**: Filter by progress stage

## 🚀 **Setup Process**

### **Step 1: Connect Notion Database**
1. Sign up for MapOn.ai account
2. In MapOn.ai dashboard, select "Connect Data Source"
3. Choose "Notion" integration
4. Authorize access to your workspace
5. Select specific databases:
   - Mapa de Distribuição Mansão Maromba
   - Alvos de Prospecção
   - Backlog Radar Mansão Maromba (if location-relevant)

### **Step 2: Configure Mapping**
For each database, assign fields:
- **Address/Location Field**: Choose the field containing addresses
- **Size Field**: Volume, rating, or priority for bubble sizing
- **Color Field**: Status, type, or funnel stage for color coding
- **Label Field**: Business name or prospect name for map labels

### **Step 3: Customize & Share**
- **Map Styles**: Choose from various base maps (street, satellite, terrain)
- **Filters**: Add interactive filters for status, type, region
- **Layers**: Overlay multiple databases (distribution + prospects)
- **Public Links**: Generate shareable URLs for team access
- **Notion Embeds**: Copy map links to Notion pages for live previews
